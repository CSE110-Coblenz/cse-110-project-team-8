About The Game

VimMaster is a practice ground for Vim, developed by a group of students in CSE 110 at UCSD.

Move

Use Vim arrows: h/j/k/l or the arrow keys.

Type

Press i for insert mode, type characters, Esc to return to normal.

Exit

To pause or exit the game, press F9 to see the result.

Goals

Match the expected grid on the right. Your score will be determined on your accuracy.
